Our History

Since 2009,  Mohona Packages BD limited has been viewed upon to be the ultimate source of providing backward linkage support to the country's bustling export-oriented RMG sector.
Our factory utilizes modern technologies and semi-automatic machinery to produce the finest quality products. The machines are all from Taiwan, China and India.

We also established a new state of art automated polymer facility with machines from Taiwan, which produces over 4000 kilograms of the highest grade quality poly bags.  And in 2022, we had set up an extension brand new printing  to offer even more from our expanding catalogue.

Our factory stretches over 52,200 square feet with 120 employees. Our versatile workforce is well equipped to handle all our sort of customer demands.
